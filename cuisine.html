    North Indian Cuisine

    North India is known for its rice, aromatic, and flavorful dishes ofeten cooked with ghee and spices:
  • Biryani: A fragrant rice dish made with aromatic spices amd meat or vegetable.
  • Butter Chicken: A creamy, tomato-based curry that's a favorite worldwide.
  • Paneer Tikka: Marinated paneer cubes grilled to perfection

    south Indian Cuisine

    South India offers a wide array of dishes with a perfect blend of a spices and tangy flavors:
  • Msala Dosa: A crispy rice stuffed with spicy potato filling.
  • Idli and Sambar: Soft rice cakes served with a tangy lentil-based vegetable stew.
  • Chettinad Chicken: A fiery, flavorful chicken dish from Tamil Nadu.
    • South Indian favorite: Masala Dosa

    Sweet Delights

    No Indian meal is complete without indulging in some mouth-watering dessers:
  • Gulab Jambun: fried milk solids soaked in sugar syrup.
  • Rasgulla: Soft and spongy cheese balls in light sugar syrup.
  • Payasam: A South Indian dessert made of milk, rice, and jaggery.
